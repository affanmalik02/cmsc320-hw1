What is the difference between a merge and a rebase?

A merge uploads your local branch to the main branch,
while a rebase is for when you want to update the start of your branch to a later commit that happend after you branched out.

What is a remote repository?

A remote repository is a central online repository that is seen by several collaborators, 
and one that is separate from your local repository, typically where you maintain the central code for a team.
